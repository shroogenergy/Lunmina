Lumina Terra – Visionary Clean Energy Dome

Overview

Lumina Terra is a pioneering architectural and energy innovation project that merges sustainable clean energy, education, and light technology under a dome-shaped building. Aligned with NEOM’s futuristic vision, the project combines hydrogen, solar, AI, and etheric energy technologies into an immersive space for public benefit, training, and global impact.

Core Technology

  • Horomnic Energy System: A conceptual model inspired by zero-point and etheric energy.
  • Transparent Luminescent Solar Concentrators (TLSC): Energy-harvesting glass to power the structure naturally.
  • Hydrogen Integration: Embedded smart hydrogen storage and green hydrogen workshops.
  • AI-Powered Optimization: Smart system management for climate, lighting, and user flow.

Structure Components

  • Interactive Dome: Main structure acting as an energy hub and educational space.
  • Learning Zones: Modular labs, conference areas, meditation spaces, and innovation corridors.
  • Energy Garden: Solar sculptures and hydrogen fountains.

Target Audience

  • NEOM developers & urban planners
  • Clean energy researchers & startups
  • Global sustainability investors
  • Students, visitors, and tourists seeking futuristic experiences

Revenue Model

  • Hosting global summits and private energy events
  • Offering exclusive clean energy and AI training only available at the site
  • Retail and virtual experience platforms
  • Tech licensing and international partnerships

Strategic Goal

To establish Lumina Terra as a flagship NEOM dome and global model for clean energy innovation, while positioning the founder as a partner and visionary contributor to Saudi Arabia’s energy future.
